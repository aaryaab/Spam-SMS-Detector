Spam SMS Detector
 
 Spam: Spam SMS
 Ham : Non Spam SMS

Flow is as below:
1) Data Cleaning 
   - Text Preprocessing
  
WordCloud is created to see common words in Spam and Ham message
 

2) Data Modeling:
  - Logistic Regression is used
  - Naive Bayes is used
  
To improvise the models, Stemming, TFIDF Vectorizer is applied on the cleaned data
